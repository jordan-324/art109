about

Jordan Medina is a multimedia artist from the San Francisco Bay Area. He is currently working toward a Bachelor in Fine Arts in Digital Media Arts at San Jose State University. Medina works across various mediums ranging from painting, photography, video, installation and digital processes. His practice seeks to document, question, and preserve. Medina’s work is a conversation between place, memory, and the strange intersections of human life.
