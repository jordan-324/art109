photo—Diary (2025)

Webpage

Visit the live site →

photo—Diary is an extension of my phone camera roll, presented as a public-facing webpage. The project explores everyday image-making and personal documentation through casual, snapshot-style photography collected over many years.

Although the images are deeply personal, the structure of the site allows viewers to experience them as fragments of someone else’s visual memory—similar to scrolling through a life that is not their own.
