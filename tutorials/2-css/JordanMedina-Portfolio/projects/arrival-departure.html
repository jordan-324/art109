Arrival/Depature (2025)

Single-Channel Video

I wanted to put an emphasis on movement, specifically being in one destination and taking transportation to end up at another. I find it beautiful how much one's perception of life can change because of one's environment. In this video, I chose to capture everyday life for me while using public transportation during my first time visiting Japan. I shot various clips of me doing the same activity in different locations. I chose the train station because I frequented different stations everyday and it took up a large part of my days. I have clips of me outside the train stations, and entering the stations. I have clips of details and people in those train stations. I have clips of me entering the trains and riding the trains. I have clips of me waiting to exit the trains and having to be patient while dealing with the vast amount of people doing the same activity as me.
