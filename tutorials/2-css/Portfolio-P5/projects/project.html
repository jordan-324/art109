Missed Connections

I am a multimedia artist. I work across various mediums from video, photography, painting, and installation. At its core, my practice seeks to document, question, and preserve. Whether evoking memory, paranoia, or starting a conversation, my goal is to make viewers feel something whether it be recognition, discomfort, or laughter. My work has always been centered on representing overlooked environments, and just like the environments, it continues to evolve, examining how those spaces are shifting. My work is a conversation between place, memory, and the strange intersections of human life.
